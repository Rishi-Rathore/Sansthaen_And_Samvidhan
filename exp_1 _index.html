About Our Laws

Welcome to the Pranveer Singh Institute Of Technology, a place where innovation, creativity, and technical excellence come together to shape the future. We offer a wide range of academic programs designed to help students grow and succeed in their careers.

Our Departments

Department of Computer Science

The Department of Computer Science provides cutting-edge education in computing, programming, software development, and emerging technologies like Artificial Intelligence and Cybersecurity.

Department of Electrical Engineering

The Department of Electrical Engineering focuses on teaching students about electrical circuits, electronics, and systems. Our programs aim to prepare engineers to design and innovate for the future.

Department of Mechanical Engineering

The Mechanical Engineering Department provides expertise in design, manufacturing, and analysis of mechanical systems. We prepare students for leadership roles in the engineering field.

Department of Civil Engineering

The Civil Engineering Department offers in-depth knowledge in infrastructure development, construction, and urban planning. Students are trained to design and build sustainable structures and systems.

Department of Business Administration

The Business Administration Department equips students with the skills necessary for leadership, strategy, and management in business and entrepreneurship. We focus on developing the next generation of business leaders.
